The Digital Uplift Program

A FREE Program designed to empower Nigerian Youths, Women, Disadvantaged, Deportees, Trafficked, Internally Displaced Persons, People living with Disabilities and Gender Based Violence Survivors with contemporary and high demand digital skills training and internship so they are included in the digital economy and the future of work.

Registration is ongoing.

Program Overview

Seebuilders Innovation Hub, Seedbuilders Skills Development Foundation and Total Child Model CBT has partnered with the Ministry of Education to bridge the digital skills gap, address the impact of education disruption, access to digital innovation, improve employability/entrepreneurship and uplift 600 Marginalised Young Persons, Minority Girls and Women, Gender-Based Violence Survivors and Girls with Disabilities from Indigenous Groups in Northern Nigeria who have been previously denied access to technical, financial, and economic empowerment and are victims of abuse, gender and physical discrimination at no cost.

Uplift your career with a digital skill to be relevant for the future of work.

Unlock your potential by accessing a FREE blended learning methodology with a physical instructor led training, mentorship, peer to peer learning, soft skills bootcamp, access to an equipped computer lab, internship and earn an internationally recognized certificate!

What You'll Get

  • Get trained with a digital skill for FREE
  • Soft Skills Bootcamp
  • Internship
  • Acquire globally recognised certifications
  • Access to Freelancing and Entrepreneurship
